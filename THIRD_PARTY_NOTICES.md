Player Panel integrates with Crafty Controller, Fabric, BlueMap, and squaremap through their public APIs or Player Panel browser bridges. The web component uses `pywebpush` and `cryptography`. Each project retains its own license and trademarks.

The repository does not include Minecraft, Crafty Controller, BlueMap, squaremap, Fabric Loader, or Fabric API. When explicitly approved by the administrator, the guided installer may download compatible releases from configured official project sources.

BlueMap resource downloads remain disabled until the administrator explicitly accepts changing `accept-download` to `true`.
